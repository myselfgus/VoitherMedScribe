Frequently Asked Questions

What is Voither?

Voither is an AI-powered medical scribe that listens to patient-doctor conversations and automatically generates clinical notes, summaries, and action items, saving you valuable time.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and are compliant with all relevant data privacy regulations, including HIPAA. Your data is stored securely in the Azure cloud.

How accurate is the transcription?

Our system leverages state-of-the-art speech recognition technology, achieving over 95% accuracy in typical clinical environments. The accuracy continues to improve with more usage.
